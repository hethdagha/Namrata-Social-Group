About Us

Namrata Social Group (NSG) was founded by Nirmala Shantilal Dagha with the mission of serving those in need—humans and animals alike. With 57 dedicated women volunteers, NSG aims to inspire younger generations to join and serve.

What Do We Do?

  • Provide clothes and toys to tribal children
  • Support elderly with food, medicines, and help
  • Care for cows with shelter, fodder, and medical aid
  • Help rural hospitals with essential supplies
  • Distribute school bags, books, and stationery
  • Offer emergency assistance to families
